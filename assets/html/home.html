Welcome to Atulado Ministries

Atulado means “By Your Side” and we stand ready to support those who share the Gospel of Jesus Christ and disciple His followers.

We support the leadership of the Church in Cuba as they share the Gospel of Jesus Christ and provide resources to their communities. Some of the resources we’ve delivered include reading glasses, blood pressure cuffs, Bible study materials, and more.

On our mission trips we have two goals; deliver resources and simply “Be”. Be there to encourage those we meet, be there to listen to those eager to tell their stories, and be there to pray with those we encounter. We invite you to “Be” part of our mission.
